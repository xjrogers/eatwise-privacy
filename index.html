Privacy Policy for EatWise

Last updated: June 13, 2025

1. Information We Collect

EatWise collects the following information to provide our meal planning and nutrition services:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and authentication data
  • Usage Data: App interactions, meal preferences, and fasting schedules
  • Camera Data: Photos of food items for our Smart Scan feature (processed locally)
  • Subscription Information: Purchase history and subscription status via Apple's App Store

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized meal planning and nutrition recommendations
  • Process your subscription and manage your account
  • Improve our AI recommendations based on your preferences
  • Send important account and service updates

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored on encrypted servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. Food photos are processed locally on your device and are not stored on our servers unless you explicitly save them.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety

5. Third-Party Services

EatWise integrates with:

  • Apple App Store: For subscription management
  • OpenAI: For AI-powered meal recommendations (data is anonymized)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

7. Children's Privacy

EatWise is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@eatwiseapp.com

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes via email or in-app notification.
